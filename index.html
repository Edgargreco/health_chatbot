About HealthBot Uganda

HealthBot Uganda is an innovative AI-powered health information chatbot developed to address the growing demand for reliable, accessible healthcare information in Uganda. In today's digital age, many people seek health information online but often encounter unreliable or complex sources that can lead to confusion and anxiety.

Our chatbot leverages advanced natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning technologies to provide immediate, accurate responses to health inquiries through interactive conversations, making healthcare information more accessible for everyone.

Project Objectives

1

Literature Review & User Requirements

Conducted comprehensive literature review and field study done to identify user requirements and information needs for an AI-powered health information chatbot specifically tailored for Uganda.

2

Design & Cultural Adaptation

Chatbot is designed with features suited to address health information gaps, including natural language processing for local languages and culturally relevant content.

3

Development & Implementation

The Developed and implemented health information chatbot uses advanced AI and machine learning techniques to ensure accurate, real-time responses to users' health inquiries.

4

Testing & Validation

Tested and validated where the chatbot's performance and usability in providing accurate health information, with pilot testing was conducted across different regions in Uganda.
